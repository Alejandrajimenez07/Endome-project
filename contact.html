Living with endometriosis...


Endometriosis can be a difficult condition to deal with, both physically and emotionally.

Endometriosis affects 1.5 million women in the UK, many women feel ashamed to talk about it or try to hide this condition,
is important you understand that you are not alone.


As well as support from your doctor and family, you may find it helpful to contact our support group, for helpful information and advice.
